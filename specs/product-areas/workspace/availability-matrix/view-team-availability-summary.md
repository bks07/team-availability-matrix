# View Team Availability Summary

## Story
- **IN ORDER TO** quickly understand my team's availability at a glance
- **AS** a team member or team administrator
- **I WANT TO** see a summary row showing how many team members are Working, on Vacation, or Absent for each day

## Acceptance Criteria
- A summary row is displayed at the top or bottom of the availability matrix.
- For each day, the summary shows counts: number of members Working (W), on Vacation (V), and Absent (A).
- The summary updates dynamically when the displayed date range changes.
- The summary reflects the same data visible in the matrix (filtered by the selected team).
- The summary is visually distinct from individual user rows.

## In-Scope
- Summary row component with W/V/A counts per day.
- Dynamic calculation from visible matrix data.
- Visual distinction from regular rows.

## Out-of-Scope
- Exporting summary data.
- Percentage-based views.
- Historical trend analysis.

## Additional Information
- Useful for managers and team leads to assess team capacity for planning purposes.
