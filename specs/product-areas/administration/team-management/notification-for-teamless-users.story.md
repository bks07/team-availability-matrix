# Notification for Teamless Users

## Story
- **IN ORDER TO** inform users about their team status
- **AS** a user without a team
- **I WANT TO** see a notification that I am not part of any team

## Acceptance Criteria
- A notification is displayed for users without a team: "You are not yet part of a team. Join or create a team to collaborate."
- The notification is visible on the fallback view of the availability matrix.
- The notification includes a link to the team management page.

## In-Scope
- Notification design and placement.
- Link to the team management page.

## Out-of-Scope
- Team invitation or creation workflows.
- Advanced notification customization.

## Additional Information
- The notification ensures users are aware of their team status and can take action to join or create a team.
