# Edit Team Details

## Story
- **IN ORDER TO** update team information
- **AS** an administrator
- **I WANT TO** edit team details such as name

## Acceptance Criteria
- Admins can edit a team's name.
- The system validates that the new name is unique.
- A success message is displayed upon successful update.

## In-Scope
- Team editing form.
- Backend validation for unique team names.

## Out-of-Scope
- Assigning users to teams (covered in a separate story).

## Additional Information
- Editing team details should not affect historical data.
