# Captcha

## Story
- **IN ORDER TO** prevent automated login attempts
- **AS** a system
- **I WANT TO** require users to complete a captcha during login

## Acceptance Criteria
- Captcha is displayed after 3 failed login attempts.
- Users must complete the captcha to proceed with login.
- The system supports reCAPTCHA or similar services.

## In-Scope
- Captcha integration in the login form.
- Backend validation of captcha responses.

## Out-of-Scope
- Captcha for other forms (e.g., registration).

## Additional Information
- Captcha should be user-friendly and accessible.
